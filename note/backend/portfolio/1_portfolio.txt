for api endpoint, please follow all pattern of asset feature. but “portfolio” feature must create into new module or folder. you can Nestjs cmd to do it.
for query of “TD05_Portfolio” must always join TD01_Project.
the relationship between TD05_Portfolio and TD01_Project is many per one
for query of “TD05_Portfolio” must always join CM02_Asset.
the relationship between TD05_Portfolio and CM02_Asset is one per one
for query of “TD05_Portfolio” must always join TD03_Strategy.
the relationship between TD05_Portfolio and TD03_Strategy is one per one
